LagerLet Privacy Policy Effective date: April 7, 2026 This Privacy Policy explains how LagerLet ("we", "our", "us") collects, uses, and protects personal data when you use the LagerLet mobile application. 1. Data We Collect We may process the following categories of data: Account information: email address, user account ID, and profile details (for example name and company name). Business data: customer/product/order data synchronized from your connected e-conomic account. Authentication and access data: session tokens and security data necessary to keep you signed in. Operational data: app settings (such as language, theme, scanner settings) and local cache data. 2. Login and e-conomic Credentials LagerLet requires user authentication. Users sign in with their own account. If e-conomic credentials/tokens are provided (for example agreement grant token), they are used only to authenticate and authorize requests against e-conomic APIs. e-conomic credentials and tokens are not sold and are not shared with unrelated third parties. They are used solely for authentication/authorization and core app functionality. The global e-conomic app secret is kept server-side only and is not exposed in the mobile client. 3. How We Use Data To authenticate users and secure access to the app. To connect your account to e-conomic and process requested operations (for example read products/customers, create draft orders). To provide app features, local caching, and configuration persistence. To maintain service reliability, security, and troubleshooting. 4. Data Sharing and Processors We share data only with service providers required to operate the app: Google Firebase (authentication, database, cloud functions). e-conomic API (for user-authorized accounting/business operations). We do not sell personal data to data brokers or advertisers. 5. Storage and Security We use access controls and authenticated server-side API calls. Sensitive server credentials are stored on server infrastructure/environment variables, not in client code. Some non-sensitive app data is cached locally on device for performance (for example profile cache/settings). 6. Retention We retain data only as long as needed for legitimate business and legal purposes, or until deletion is requested and permitted by applicable law. 7. Your Rights Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your data. To request this, contact us at the address below. 8. Children LagerLet is intended for business use and is not directed to children. 9. Changes to This Policy We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updated versions will be published with a revised effective date. 10. Contact Contact us at: support@lagerlet.com If you use a different support email, replace this before publishing. Play Console Note This app uses user login (not anonymous global client key usage). e-conomic authorization tokens are tied to authenticated users/companies and processed for core functionality. The server-side app secret token is not embedded in the mobile app.
